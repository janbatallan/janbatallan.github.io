Graphic Designer

The path of graphic designing has always been one of interest to me. It requires the ability to work well with computer programs like Photoshop and Illustrator. You also need to know how to incorporate design principles to make your work look presentable. I believe I have thse skills, and could make a good use of them in the future in this career path.

Architect

Architecture involves skills that I feel I am good at. You need good concepts of designing buildings and houses, and you need to stay updated in the structure styles of the current time period. Since I was small, I have enjoyed building things such as model houses, and making 3D dioramas. I find contruction its own form of art. It is very nice to see how the designs of buildings can be so aesthetically pleasing.

Photographer

Photography started off as more of a hobby to me. Taking pictures frequently has become more of a habit to me. With photography, you can use your creativity to photograph virtually anything in the world to make it look interesting. You can also use digital programs like Lightroom to enhance the quality of your photographs. I hadn't thought of it in the past, but now I find it more interesting.

Crime Scene Investigator

I find investigating mysteries very interesting. Being a crime scene investigator and figuring out the thoughts behind crimes would be an insight to another world. The reason this career isn't my top choice is because a career like this would take a huge mental toll on you. Some crimes have very dark causes and outcomes, and seeing them on a daily basis might be difficult.
